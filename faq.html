Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our services

General Questions

What is a Sacco?

A Sacco (Savings and Credit Cooperative) is a member-owned financial institution that provides savings accounts, loans, and other financial services to its members. Unlike banks, Sacco's primary goal is to serve their members rather than make profits for shareholders.

Why should I join Bali Baseeka Sacco?

Bali Baseeka Sacco offers competitive interest rates on savings, low-interest loans, financial education, and a community of like-minded savers. With over 5,000 members and ₦50M+ in savings, we are a trusted partner in your financial journey.

How do I become a member?

To become a member, you need to: 1) Fill out our membership application form, 2) Provide valid ID and proof of residence, 3) Pay the minimum initial deposit of ₦5,000, and 4) Receive your membership card. You can apply online or visit our office.

Savings & Accounts

What is the minimum amount to open a savings account?

The minimum amount to open a savings account is ₦1,000. However, to become a full member with voting rights, you need to make an initial deposit of at least ₦5,000.

What interest rate do you offer on savings?

We offer competitive interest rates ranging from 8% to 12% per annum, depending on your account type and balance. Fixed deposits can earn up to 15% interest. Interest is calculated monthly and credited to your account quarterly.

Can I withdraw my savings anytime?

Yes, you can withdraw from your savings account at any time through our mobile banking app, online portal, or by visiting our office. However, please note that maintaining a minimum balance helps maximize your interest earnings.

Loans & Credit

What are the loan eligibility requirements?

To qualify for a loan, you must be: 1) An active member for at least 3 months, 2) Have a good savings record, 3) Have a regular income source, 4) Provide required security/guarantors, and 5) Have no outstanding default loans.

How long does it take to get a loan approved?

Loan approval typically takes 24-72 hours from the time you submit all required documents. Smaller loans with minimal documentation can be approved within 24 hours. Larger loans may take longer depending on the amount and security provided.

What is the maximum loan amount I can get?

Members can borrow up to 3 times their savings balance for regular loans. For special loans with collateral, the amount can go up to ₦5,000,000. Group loans can have higher limits based on group size and collective savings.

What is the interest rate on loans?

Our loan interest rates start from 1.5% per month (18% annual rate) for members with excellent savings records. Rates may vary based on loan type, amount, and security provided. Contact our loans department for specific rates.

Mobile Banking & Technology

How do I register for mobile banking?

To register for mobile banking, download our app from the Google Play Store or visit our office. You'll need your membership number and a valid ID. Our staff will assist you with the registration process and set up your secure PIN.

Is mobile banking secure?

Yes, our mobile banking platform uses bank-grade 256-bit encryption and secure authentication methods. We also provide transaction alerts and you can enable biometric login (fingerprint/face) for added security.
