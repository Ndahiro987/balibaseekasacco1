What We Offer

Explore Our Services

At Bali Baseeka Sacco, we provide a wide range of financial products and services tailored to help our members achieve their financial goals.

Savings Account

Secure your future with our high-yield savings accounts. Start saving with as little as UGX 1,000 and watch your money grow with competitive interest rates.

  • Competitive interest rates
  • No minimum balance after initial deposit
  • Easy withdrawal access
  • Free monthly statements
  • ATM card available

Loans & Credit

Access quick, affordable loans for business, education, housing, or personal needs with flexible repayment plans.

  • Interest rates as low as 1.5% per month
  • Quick approval (24-72 hours)
  • Flexible repayment up to 36 months
  • Loan amounts from UGX 50,000 to UGX 50,000,000
  • Security/collateral required for loans
  • Apply for Loan

Group Lending (Chama)

Join with friends, family, or colleagues to access larger loans through our trusted group lending program.

  • Shared responsibility among members
  • Higher loan limits for groups
  • Lower interest rates
  • Group training and financial education
  • Regular group meetings support

Fixed Deposit

Earn higher returns on your savings with our fixed deposit accounts. Lock in your funds for higher interest rates.

  • Higher interest rates (up to 15%)
  • Flexible tenure options (3-24 months)
  • Automatic renewal options
  • Loan against deposit available
  • Interest paid monthly or quarterly

Insurance Services

Protect your savings and family with our comprehensive insurance products designed specifically for sacco members.

  • Life insurance cover
  • Asset protection
  • Funeral cover
  • Medical insurance
  • Affordable premiums

Mobile Banking

Manage your account anytime, anywhere with our secure mobile banking platform. Check balance, transfer, and pay bills.

  • 24/7 account access
  • Instant fund transfers
  • Bill payments
  • Transaction alerts
  • USSD code available

Financial Education

Attend our free workshops and seminars to improve your financial literacy and make smarter money decisions.

  • Free financial workshops
  • Business management training
  • Investment guidance
  • Budgeting tips
  • Entrepreneurship support

Youth & Women Programs

Special financial products and training programs designed to empower youth and women entrepreneurs.

  • Youth savings accounts
  • Women entrepreneurship loans
  • Mentorship programs
  • Business development training
  • Networking opportunities
