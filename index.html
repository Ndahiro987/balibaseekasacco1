Who We Are

About Bali Baseeka Sacco

We are a community-driven savings and credit cooperative committed to uplifting our members through sustainable financial solutions.

Our Mission

To provide accessible financial services that empower our members to achieve economic independence and collective growth.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted cooperative financial institution, delivering excellence in savings and credit services.

Our Values

Transparency, integrity, member focus, teamwork, and social responsibility guide everything we do.

Loans & Credit

Become a Member

How to Join

  1. Fill out the membership form
  2. Submit required documents
  3. Pay initial deposit (₦5,000 minimum)
  4. Receive your membership card
  5. Start enjoying benefits!
Get Started

Member Benefits

  • Access to low-interest loans
  • Annual dividends on savings
  • Financial planning assistance
  • Training and development
  • Voting rights in general meetings

Requirements

  • Valid ID (National ID, Passport, etc.)
  • Proof of residence
  • Passport photograph
  • 2 references from members
  • Minimum age: 18 years
