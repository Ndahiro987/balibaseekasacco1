Loan Calculator FAQ

How is interest calculated?

Interest is calculated on a reducing balance basis (diminishing balance). This means interest is charged only on the remaining principal balance each month.

Can I pay off my loan early?

Yes! You can make early repayments without penalties. This will reduce the total interest paid over the loan term.

What affects my interest rate?

Your interest rate depends on your membership duration, savings record, loan type, and the security/collateral provided.

How much can I borrow?

Members can borrow up to 3 times their savings balance. With collateral, higher amounts may be available.
