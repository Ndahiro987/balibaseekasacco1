How It Works

Loan Application Process

Please follow these steps to successfully apply for a loan

Step 1: Register

Become a member by completing the membership registration form and paying the initial deposit.

Step 2: Download Form

Download the loan application form from our downloads page after registering as a member.

Step 3: Fill & Submit

Fill out the form and attach your security documents (see below). Bring all documents to our office.

Step 4: Visit Office

Bring the completed form and original security documents to our office for review and processing.

Required Documents

Security/Collateral Documents

You must provide the following documents when applying for a loan

Proof of Identity

Valid National ID, Passport, or Driver's License (original and copy)

Proof of Residence

Utility bill, letter from local council, or rental agreement

Land Title (if applicable)

Title deed, lease agreement, or land registration documents for collateral

Vehicle Title (if applicable)

Logbook, registration documents, or insurance certificate for vehicle collateral

Proof of Income

Pay slips, bank statements, or business registration documents

Guarantor

Details of a member willing to act as guarantor with their ID and savings details

Ready to Apply?

After registering as a member, download the loan application form, fill it out, and bring it to our office along with your security documents.
