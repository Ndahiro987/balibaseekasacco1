Our Story

Building Financial Resilience Since 2009

Bali Baseeka Sacco was established with a vision to provide accessible financial services to individuals and communities who traditionally had limited access to formal banking services. What started as a small group of committed savers has grown into a trusted financial institution serving thousands of members across the region.

Our name "Bali Baseeka" comes from the local dialect, meaning "unity" or "coming together" - reflecting our core philosophy that collective strength leads to individual prosperity.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, affordable, and sustainable financial services that empower our members to achieve economic independence and improve their quality of life through collective savings and responsible lending.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted cooperative financial institution in the region, recognized for excellence in member service, financial sustainability, and positive community impact.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We conduct our business with honesty and transparency
  • Member Focus: Our members are at the heart of everything we do
  • Teamwork: We achieve more by working together
  • Excellence: We strive for continuous improvement
  • Social Responsibility: We give back to our community
Our Journey

Milestones & Achievements

2009

Foundation

Bali Baseeka Sacco was officially registered with 50 founding members and initial capital of UGX 500,000.

2012

First Branch

Opened our first satellite office to serve members in the eastern district.

2015

1,000 Members

Reached the milestone of 1,000 active members and ₦10M in savings portfolio.

2018

Mobile Banking

Launched our mobile banking platform, enabling members to access services remotely.

2021

Digital Transformation

Implemented core banking system and online membership applications.

2024

5,000+ Members

Proudly serving over 5,000 members with ₦50M+ in savings and assets.
